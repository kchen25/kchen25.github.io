About Me

Hello! I'm a first-year at Harvard studying CS and math. My interest in computer science began in 2018 when I started developing a mobile app for my high school newspaper. As an artist and math enthusiast, I found coding to be the perfect intersection between logic and creativity. Since then, I've conducted UI/UX research with Harvard Student Agencies, designed the website and marketing content for the largest student-run conference for women engineers, and am part of the web team for Datamatch, a satirical college matchmaking service with over 40,000 users.
